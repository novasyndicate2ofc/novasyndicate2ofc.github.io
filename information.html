✦ Welcome

Welcome to NOVA SYNDICATE.

✦ Server Purpose

NOVA SYNDICATE was created to provide an inclusive competitive environment where players of all ranks have the opportunity to grow and compete. We focus on development, teamwork, and maintaining a respectful atmosphere.

✦ Membership
  • Open to players of all ranks and skill levels
  • Activity and positive attitude are required
  • Respect toward staff and members is expected
✦ Tryouts

Tryouts are announced periodically in the appropriate channel. When open, details regarding requirements, scheduling, and evaluation criteria will be posted.

✦ Activity Expectations

Members are expected to:

  • Stay active within the server
  • Participate in team activities when available
  • Maintain good sportsmanship
✦ Staff Structure

Our staff team ensures the server remains organized, safe, and professional. All moderation decisions are made to protect the integrity of NOVA SYNDICATE.

✦ Community Values

NOVA SYNDICATE is built on respect, growth, and opportunity. Toxicity, discrimination, and disruptive behavior are not tolerated.

We are committed to building not only strong competitors, but a strong community.
